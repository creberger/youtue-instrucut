Collatz Conjecture

Does your number reach 1?

The Collatz conjecture is one of mathematics' most famous unsolved problems. It claims that for any positive integer, the sequence below will always eventually reach 1.

If the number is even → divide by 2  |  If the number is odd → multiply by 3 and add 1  |  Repeat until you reach 1
